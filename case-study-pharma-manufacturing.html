Executive Summary

A pharmaceutical and medical-equipment manufacturer with 225 employees needed to run its full production lifecycle — raw materials to finished goods — under strict regulatory compliance, while consolidating three separate systems into one. I architected a single Odoo ERP with a custom shopfloor, a revamped quality process at every production stage, capacity-based multi-warehouse inventory, and a Tally integration for accounting migration. Production-tracking errors fell by 50%.

The Challenges

  • Production, inventory and accounting spread across three disconnected systems.
  • Complex manufacturing flows — batch generation, split work orders, unbuild logic, and a samples workflow — not supported out of the box.
  • Quality control needed at every production stage for regulatory compliance.
  • Inventory across 5+ warehouses with storage-capacity constraints.
  • Accounting history locked inside Tally and needing migration into Odoo.

Solution Architecture

[ Odoo ERP ] -- single source of truth
   |-- Manufacturing (custom Shopfloor: batches, split WO, unbuild, samples)
   |-- Quality (QC checks at every production stage)
   |-- Inventory (capacity-based, 5+ warehouses)
   |-- Sales (Quotation -> Proforma -> Sale Order -> Tax Invoice)
   |-- Purchase / HR / Payroll / Attendance / Maintenance / Repairs
   |-- Dashboards (cost per employee / process / department)
        ^
   [Tally] --XML integration--> accounting migration into Odoo

Key Customizations

  • Custom Shopfloor module — bespoke production flows, batch generation, split work orders, unbuild-MO logic, and a samples workflow.
  • Quality control at every stage — revamped QC checkpoints across the production process for regulatory compliance.
  • Structured sales workflow — Quotation → Proforma → Sale Order → Tax Invoice.
  • Capacity-based inventory — storage-capacity-aware management across 5+ warehouse locations.
  • Tally XML integration — migrated full accounting into Odoo, consolidating three systems into one.
  • Cost analytics dashboards — cost per employee, per process and per department.
