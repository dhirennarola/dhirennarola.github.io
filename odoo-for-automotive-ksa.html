The Problem

What Automotive Businesses in KSA Struggle With

ZATCA Phase 2 compliance

Mandatory e-invoicing with XML, QR codes and clearance — a hard deadline, with penalties for getting it wrong.

Complex payroll

Overtime, violations, shifts with approvals and end-of-service across branches under Saudi labour law.

Disconnected online store

A WooCommerce spare-parts store running separately from stock and accounting, with manual reconciliation.

Manual customer service

Thousands of service requests handled by phone, with slow responses and no automation.

The Solution

How Odoo Solves It

  • ZATCA Phase 2 e-invoicing — compliant XML, QR codes and clearance, embedded in standard Odoo accounting (proven at 1,500+ invoices/month).
  • Saudi labour-law payroll — overtime, violations, shift approvals and end-of-service across branches, plus an employee self-service portal.
  • WooCommerce integration — product, stock and order sync between your spare-parts store and Odoo.
  • WhatsApp helpdesk — automated, multi-channel customer service that cut ticket response time ~40%.
  • Multi-company, multi-branch accounting, sales, purchase and inventory in one system.

FAQ

Common Questions

Does Odoo support ZATCA Phase 2 e-invoicing?
Yes. With the right configuration and customization, Odoo generates ZATCA Phase 2 compliant XML invoices with QR codes and handles clearance, embedded in the standard accounting flow. I've delivered this in production for a 300+ employee automotive group issuing 1,500+ invoices a month.
Can Odoo handle Saudi labour-law payroll?
Yes — including overtime and violation management, shift management with approvals, and end-of-service calculations per local regulations, across multiple branches.
Can you connect our existing WooCommerce store?
Yes. I sync products, stock and orders between WooCommerce and Odoo so your online spare-parts sales flow straight into inventory and accounting.
