My Approach

How I Work

A structured, ownership-first process — so you always know what's happening, and there are no handoff surprises.

1

Discovery & Architecture

I start where most Odoo projects go wrong: understanding your business before touching the system. Requirements, gap analysis, and a signed-off solution architecture — so we build the right thing once.

2

Sprint-Based Implementation

Configuration, custom development, and integrations delivered in structured sprints with proactive updates. AI-assisted workflows keep delivery roughly 25% faster, with fewer iterations and less rework.

3

Go-Live & Handover

Data migration, UAT, and a calm go-live — with documentation and team training so you own the system. No black-box implementations, no single point of dependency.

4

Post-Go-Live Support

SLA-based support after launch, because ERP needs evolve as the business grows. One accountable point of contact who knows your system — and replies within 8 business hours.
