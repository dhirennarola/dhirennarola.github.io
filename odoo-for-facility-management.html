Odoo for Facility Management — Service That Runs Itself.

Maintenance companies live and die by scheduling and customer communication. I build Odoo systems that tie subscriptions to automated maintenance visits and let thousands of customers self-serve over WhatsApp — proven to replace half a call-centre team.

The Problem

What Slows Facility & Maintenance Teams Down

Manual scheduling

Visits planned by hand, leading to missed service dates and unhappy subscribers.

Call-centre overload

A large team answering subscription, payment and service queries by phone, all day.

Renewal & billing leakage

Subscriptions and payments tracked loosely, so renewals slip and revenue leaks.

No technician visibility

Field workforce attendance and dispatch disconnected from the back office.

The Solution

How Odoo Solves It

  • Subscription + Maintenance — plans tied to automated maintenance scheduling, so visits happen on time without manual planning.
  • WhatsApp self-service — customers handle subscriptions, visit reports, requests and payments themselves, replacing a large share of call-centre work.
  • Attendance integration — real-time technician tracking from physical devices (e.g. LanaTime).
  • Reliable notifications — WhatsApp with SMS fallback for near-total reach.
  • CRM & Contacts — one customer record across sales, service and billing.

Proof: elevator maintenance & subscription company

WhatsApp self-service for 4,000+ customers replaced ~50% of the call centre; missed visits cut 35%; 98% notification rate.
