Executive Summary

A leading automotive servicing and spare-parts group in Saudi Arabia needed a complete ERP for 300+ employees across multiple companies and branches. The existing setup relied on disconnected spreadsheets and outdated accounting. I architected and delivered a full Odoo implementation — HR, payroll, accounting, sales, purchase, inventory and helpdesk — with ZATCA Phase 2 e-invoicing, a WooCommerce store integration, and WhatsApp automation. Manual processing dropped by roughly 60%, and the business now issues 1,500+ compliant invoices a month without adding headcount.

Client Background

The client operates premium car servicing alongside a high-volume spare-parts resale business across multiple branches in Saudi Arabia. Growth had outpaced their tools: HR and payroll lived in spreadsheets, accounting sat in a legacy system, and the online spare-parts store ran separately on WooCommerce with no link to stock or accounting.

Client name withheld under NDA. Reference available privately on request.

The Challenges

  • Payroll for 300+ employees across branches managed manually, with complex overtime, violation and end-of-service rules under Saudi labour law.
  • A ZATCA Phase 2 e-invoicing mandate approaching with no compliant capability in place.
  • A WooCommerce spare-parts store disconnected from inventory and accounting.
  • Thousands of customer service requests handled manually, with slow response times.
  • No single source of truth — accounting, HR and sales data lived in separate systems.

Requirements

  • Consolidated multi-company, multi-branch ERP for 300+ employees.
  • ZATCA-compliant e-invoicing (Phase 2) integrated into accounting.
  • HR & payroll with Saudi labour-law overtime, violations, shifts, approvals and end-of-service.
  • Employee self-service portal and structured appraisal workflows.
  • WooCommerce ↔ Odoo product, stock and order synchronisation.
  • A multi-channel helpdesk with WhatsApp automation.

Solution Architecture

Odoo became the single backbone, with the storefront, e-invoicing clearance and messaging connected through it:

Key Customizations

  • ZATCA Phase 2 e-invoicing — compliant XML invoice generation, QR codes and clearance, embedded into the standard accounting flow (1,500+ invoices/month).
  • Saudi labour-law payroll — overtime and violation management, shift management with approvals, and an end-of-service module per local regulations.
  • Employee self-service portal and custom appraisal workflows with bonus and allowance management.
  • WooCommerce integration — product, stock and order sync between the online spare-parts store and Odoo (1,000+ orders/month).
  • WhatsApp helpdesk bot — a multi-channel helpdesk with WhatsApp automation that cut ticket response time by ~40%.
