The Problem

The Challenges Manufacturers Face With ERP

Disconnected systems

Production, inventory and accounting in separate tools, so no one number can be trusted end-to-end.

Quality gaps

QC happens at the end, not at each stage — so defects and compliance issues are caught too late.

Rigid out-of-the-box flows

Batches, split work orders, unbuild logic and samples rarely fit standard MRP without customization.

Multi-warehouse blind spots

Stock spread across locations with capacity limits and no real-time visibility into what's where.

No cost visibility

You can't see cost per process, per product or per department — so margins are a guess.

Migration fear

Years of data locked in Tally or legacy tools, with no safe path into a modern ERP.

The Solution

How Odoo Solves It

The right modules, configured and customized to how your plant actually runs.

  • Manufacturing (MRP) — BOMs, work orders, and a custom shopfloor for batches, split work orders, unbuild logic and samples.
  • Quality — QC checkpoints at every production stage for compliance and fewer defects.
  • Inventory — capacity-aware, multi-warehouse stock with real-time visibility.
  • Accounting + Dashboards — cost per employee, per process and per department, in real time.
  • Purchase & Sales — structured quotation → proforma → order → tax invoice flows.
  • Migration — safe data migration from Tally or legacy systems, consolidating everything into one ERP.

Proof: 225-employee pharma manufacturer

3 systems consolidated into 1 Odoo ERP, custom shopfloor & QC, 5+ warehouses, Tally integration — production-tracking errors cut 50%.
