Executive Summary

An IT services company needed to sell and track work in hourly packages without the manual billing overhead. I built a subscription-based hourly-billing system on Odoo: customers buy monthly hour packages that auto-allocate to projects and auto-renew on payment, with a self-service portal to track consumed hours and buy more. Manual billing was eliminated entirely and client queries dropped 45%.

The Challenges

  • Hourly work billed manually each month — slow and error-prone.
  • Clients had no visibility into consumed hours or task progress, generating constant queries.
  • Documents and backups depended on default Odoo.sh filestore limits.
  • Customer onboarding needed verification and fraud protection.
  • Purchases needed multi-level approval controls.

Key Customizations

  • Subscription hourly billing — monthly hour packages with automatic allocation to projects and auto-renewal on payment, eliminating 100% of manual billing.
  • Self-service customer portal — track consumed hours and task progress, and buy extra hours directly from sale orders with real-time project allocation (−45% client queries).
  • Backblaze storage integration — all documents, attachments and automated database backups moved off the default Odoo.sh filestore.
  • Secure onboarding — multi-step verification with automatic fraud detection, strong password policy and mandatory 2FA.
  • Multi-level purchase approvals for controlled procurement.
